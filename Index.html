Our Story

Where nature
meets craft

Organikaa was born from a love of natural textures and the belief that a home should tell a story. Every macramé knot, every curve of driftwood, every lamp cast in reclaimed timber is shaped entirely by hand — no two pieces are ever the same.

Based in Dubai, we create for homes across the UAE and the world — for people who want their spaces to feel alive, rooted, and deeply personal.

How We Work

The craft
process

01
Source

We gather natural cotton cord, driftwood pieces, and reclaimed timber — materials chosen for character, not uniformity.

02
Design

Each piece begins as a sketch, then a tension test. The design evolves as the material guides it — we follow the wood's grain, the rope's drape.

03
Craft

Every knot is tied by hand. Every lamp is assembled, wired, and finished by the same artisan who started it — start to end, no shortcuts.

04
Deliver

Wrapped in tissue, packed with care, and shipped to your door — or collected from our Dubai studio. We include a care card with every piece.
